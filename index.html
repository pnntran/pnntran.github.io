About

Hi there, and welcome! My name is Phuong Tran. For more than a decade, I have been fueled by a passion to translate complex ideas into cohesive branded products in areas of health, science and education. My work is in user interface and user experience design. Driven by a personal commitment to deliver quality-oriented visual solutions, my work covers creative approaches across design and development. This includes website designs, mobile applications, social media campaigns, infographics, and publication reports from my work experience, my Master's program, and my current pursuit with the MIT xPRO's Professional Certificate in Coding.

I graduated from Virginia Polytechnic Institute and Technology with my Bachelors in 2011 and completed my Masters in Design Management and Communications at Georgetown University in 2021. My current endeavor is in grasping full-stack development with MERN through the MIT xPRO's Professional Certificate in Coding. By day, I continue to work as a Senior UI/UX Designer and by night, I am in training to become a Full-Stack Coder.

This site covers some JavaScript projects I've been dabbing in with the program. Visit my GitHub for more: pnntran.
